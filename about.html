Careers

US Army: 2011 - 2019
Bethesda, MD: 2014 - 2019
I was stationed in Bethesda, Maryland working at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center (WRNMMC) as a medical laboratory technician (MLT) in the clinical microsopy section in the department of pathology.
My roles involved training incoming students or new staff members on how to use the instruments and analyzers; running patient specimens, transferring patient results from the instruments to computer software; performing, reviewing, and logging quality control (QC) data performed on the instruments; and sometimes calibrating.

Vilsesck, Germany: 2012 - 2014
I was a medical laboratory specialist serving with the 2nd Calvary Regiment (2CR), Regiment Support Squadron (RSS). I was able to set up tents and set up my own laboratory station.
I was also working at US Army Health Vilseck Clinic to continue practicing my lab skills.
During months of soldiers getting ready to deploy or coming back from deployment, I assited in the Soldier Readiness Pre-Deployment/Post-Deployment (SRP) by training army medics in phlebotomy to transport the specimens to the lab for testing.
